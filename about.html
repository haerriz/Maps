Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies including Leaflet.js for mapping, OpenStreetMap for data, and free APIs for real-time information. Our commitment to using open-source and free services ensures Haerriz Maps remains accessible to all users.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy matters. We use minimal data collection, no tracking cookies, and all location data is processed locally in your browser. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
