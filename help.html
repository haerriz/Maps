Help & Support

Find answers and get the most out of Haerriz Maps

Getting Started

How do I plan a trip?

Simply click on the map to add stops, or use the search box to find specific locations. The AI will automatically optimize your route and provide real-time traffic updates.

How does the AI optimization work?

Our AI analyzes traffic patterns, weather conditions, road types, and your preferences to suggest the most efficient route. It considers factors like travel time, fuel consumption, and road conditions.

Features & Tools

Can I use Haerriz Maps offline?

Yes! Haerriz Maps is a Progressive Web App that works offline. Install it on your device for the best experience. Some features like real-time traffic require an internet connection.

How accurate is the traffic data?

We use real-time traffic analysis based on road types, time of day, and geographic factors. While we strive for accuracy, always check current conditions before traveling.

What transport modes are supported?

Haerriz Maps supports driving, walking, cycling, and public transit (where available). You can switch between modes or use mixed-mode planning for complex journeys.

Technical Support

Why can't I access my location?

Location access may be blocked by your browser or device settings. Check your browser permissions or use the search function to manually set your starting location.

The map is not loading properly

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If problems persist, try using a different browser.

How do I export my trip?

Click the "Export" button in the actions section to download your trip as a JSON file. This includes all stops, routes, and travel information.

Privacy & Data

What data do you collect?

We collect minimal data and prioritize your privacy. Location data is processed locally in your browser. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is Haerriz Maps free to use?

Yes, Haerriz Maps is completely free with all features included. We use open-source technologies and free APIs to keep the service accessible to everyone.
