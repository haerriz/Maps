Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Last updated: January 2, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Haerriz Maps is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Location Data: Only when you explicitly grant permission, processed locally in your browser
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information for compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide route planning and navigation services
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure technical functionality and security

3. Data Storage and Processing

  • Location data is processed locally in your browser
  • No personal data is stored on our servers
  • Trip data is saved locally on your device only

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following free, open-source services:

  • OpenStreetMap: Map data and tiles
  • Nominatim: Geocoding services
  • OSRM: Route calculation
  • Various Weather APIs: Weather information

5. Cookies and Local Storage

  • We use minimal cookies for app functionality
  • Local storage saves your preferences and trip data
  • No tracking or advertising cookies

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (clear browser storage)
  • Control location permissions
  • Opt-out of analytics

7. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • No sensitive data stored on servers
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@haerriz.com
  • Address: Haerriz Technologies, India

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
